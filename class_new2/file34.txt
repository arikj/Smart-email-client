Hello everyone,

Yahoo! in collaboration with ACA (Association for Computing Activities) is
organizing HackU in IIT Kanpur from Aug 22 to Aug 25. The event will kick
off tomorrow (22 Aug.) at 6pm in LHC (L16). You can visit
http://www.cse.iitk.ac.in/users/aca/Hacku/ for the schedule and more info.
To register visit http://in.hacku.yahoo.com/register.php. Please note that
registration is mandatory for participation. The event is open to all
students, irrespective of department and batch.

About Yahoo! HackU
Yahoo! HackU is a worldwide app development competition held in technical
universities across the world. With the web experts from Yahoo! there to
help, it’ll be an enjoyable ride for everyone, with a lot of learning,
coding and even more fun. And this time it promises to pack an even bigger
bang! Bigger prizes, better food, and a cool new arsenal of Yahoo products
to make your Hack with!
Prizes include i-pads, samsung smartphones, wireless hard disks and
raspberry pis. All the teams that demo their hack on 25th will get HackU
T-shirts.
