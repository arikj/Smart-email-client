ACA and Programming Club are organizing the first WPC (Weekend Programming
Contest) for this semester.


Contest Description:  It is a 24 hours programming contest based on
ACM-ICPC format containing 15 problems on various areas.

Contest Time: August 17, 18:00 hrs (Saturday) to August 18, 18:00 hrs
(Sunday).
As this will be a team contest, you need to register your team on
http://www2.cse.iitk.ac.in/judge/public/. Registration will begin on
10:00 hrs on August 17.

The link for the contest would be posted on the ACA Facebook page (
https://www.facebook.com/aca.cse.iitk) and the Programming Club Facebook
group (https://www.facebook.com/groups/pclubiitk) well before the contest.

There will be prizes as well for top 3 teams.

Hoping for an enthusiastic participation. Have fun coding !
